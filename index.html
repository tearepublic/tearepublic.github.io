FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

When will I recieve my box of tea?

If you have subscribed in the first half of the month (before the 15th), expect your first and consecutive boxes to be delivered in the middle of the month. Otherwise, your box will be delivered at the end of each month.

How will i get charged?

Your card will get charged immediately and then you will get charged on a purchase date each month.

Will tea box fit through my mailbox?

Yes, you don't have to be home for the delivery. We have designed our packaging in the way that it can easily fit through standard mailbox.

How easy it is to cancel my subscription?

There is no commitment, you can cancel your subscription anytime online or by contacting our support team.
